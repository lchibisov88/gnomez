Stratagem limit · 11th Edition reminderCORE

Each Stratagem can only be used once per phase. In 11th Edition, a unit can only be targeted by one Stratagem per phase.

Practical note: if a Hearthguard brick uses Fury of the Hearth, it cannot also be the target of another Stratagem in that same phase.

Army Rule · Prioritised Efficiency / Yield PointsCORE

Yield Points (YP). At the end of each player's Command phase, you gain 1YP for controlling one or more objective markers within your deployment zone. From the second battle round onwards, you also gain 1YP for controlling one or more objective markers outside your deployment zone, 1YP for controlling two or more objective markers outside your deployment zone, and 1YP if you control more objective markers than your opponent controls. Your YP total cannot be reduced below 0.

Hostile Acquisition. While active, attacks made by your units that target an enemy unit within range of an objective marker have +1 to Hit. You can also re-roll Advance rolls and Charge rolls for your units.

Fortify Takeover. While active, attacks made by your units have +1 to Hit if the attacking unit is within range of an objective marker you control. In addition, each time an attack targets one of your non-VEHICLE units, if the attack's Strength is greater than that unit's Toughness, subtract 1 from the Wound roll.

Switching mode. You start the battle in Hostile Acquisition. At the end of your Command phase, if you have 7+YP, Fortify Takeover is active until the start of your next Command phase; otherwise Hostile Acquisition remains active.

Detachment Rule. Each time an enemy unit is destroyed, you gain 1YP.

How this list uses it. Hekaton Land Fortresses, Thunderkyn and Einhyr Hearthguard are the main kill-conversion pieces. Once you reach 7+YP, Fortify Takeover unlocks the defensive layer for the castle and enables the strongest Needgaârd reactive tools.

Stratagems · Core Rules PDF only

Only the Core Stratagems listed in the official 11th Edition Core Rules PDF are included here. Removed non-PDF / mission-pack extras such as New Orders, and removed 10th-edition names such as Grenade and Tank Shock. Remember: one Stratagem per target unit per phase.

Command Re-roll1CP
Core

When: Any phase, just after you make one of these rolls for a friendly unit or model: Advance, Charge, Damage, Hazard, Hit, Save, Wound, or a roll to determine the number of attacks generated with a weapon.

Target: That unit or model.

Effect: Re-roll that roll. If rolling multiple dice together, choose one die to re-roll, except Charge rolls, which are re-rolled in full.

Epic Challenge1CP
Core

When: Fight phase, just after a friendly CHARACTER unit is selected to fight.

Target: That CHARACTER unit.

Effect: Select one CHARACTER model in your unit. Until the end of the phase, that model's melee weapons have PRECISION.

Insane Bravery1CP
Core

When: Battle-shock step of your Command phase, just before you make a battle-shock roll for a friendly unit.

Target: That unit.

Effect: That battle-shock roll is automatically successful.

Restriction: Once per battle.

Explosives1CP
Core

When: Your Shooting phase.

Target: One friendly unengaged EXPLOSIVES/GRENADES unit that is eligible to shoot and did not make an Advance move this turn.

Effect: Select one EXPLOSIVES/GRENADES model in your unit, then select one unengaged visible enemy unit within 8" of that model. Roll six D6; each 4+ causes 1 mortal wound.

Crushing Impact1CP
Core

When: Your Charge phase, just after a friendly MONSTER/VEHICLE unit ends a charge move.

Target: That MONSTER/VEHICLE unit.

Effect: Select one enemy unit engaged with your unit, then one model in your unit engaged with that enemy. Roll D6 equal to that model's Toughness. Each 1 deals 1 mortal wound to your unit; each 5+ deals 1 mortal wound to the enemy unit, max 6 mortal wounds per enemy unit.

Rapid Ingress1CP
Core

When: End of your opponent's Movement phase.

Target: One friendly unit that is in Strategic Reserves, excluding AIRCRAFT.

Effect: Your unit makes an ingress move.

Restriction: Cannot be used during the first battle round.

Fire Overwatch1CP
Core

When: End of your opponent's Movement phase.

Target: One friendly unengaged unit, excluding TITANIC units.

Effect: Your unit shoots using Snap Shooting.

Snap Shooting: Target one visible eligible enemy unit within 24". Attacks only hit on unmodified 6s and cannot re-roll Hit rolls. After shooting, your unit is not eligible to start an action until the end of the phase.

Smokescreen1CP
Core

When: Start of your opponent's Shooting phase.

Target: One friendly SMOKE unit.

Effect: Until the end of the phase, attacks against that SMOKE unit, or against a unit not fully visible to the attacker because of one or more models in that SMOKE unit, treat the target as having the benefit of cover.

Heroic Intervention1CP/+1CP
Core

When: End of your opponent's Charge phase.

Target: One friendly unengaged unit within 12" of one or more enemy units. A VEHICLE target must be CHARACTER or WALKER.

Effect: Resolve a charge with your unit. Before the charge roll, choose: Leap to Defend - you can only select enemy units that made a charge move this phase and are within the maximum distance; or Into the Fray - pay +1CP, cap the charge roll at 6 after modifiers, and you can select any enemy units within 6" and within the maximum distance.

Counter-offensive2CP
Core

When: Fight step of your opponent's Fight phase, just after an enemy unit has resolved its attacks.

Target: One friendly unit that is eligible to fight.

Effect: Until the end of the phase, your unit has Fights First and must be the next unit you select to fight.
