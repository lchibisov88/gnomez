Detachment / Army Rules

English-only table reference. Detachments: Needgaârd Oathband + Farseekers. Force Dispositions: Purge the Foe + Reconnaissance.

Army Rule · Prioritised Efficiency / Yield PointsCORE

Yield Points (YP). At the end of each player’s Command phase, you gain YP for holding objectives. Your YP pool is the resource spent by Leagues of Votann rules and Stratagem riders.

Hostile Acquisition. While active, your army gets the aggressive mode: improved hit efficiency against enemies near objective markers and mobility help through Advance/Charge reliability.

Fortify Takeover. While active, your army shifts into the defensive/board-control mode: attacks from your units are more accurate when the attacker is near an objective you control; non-VEHICLE units also get the defensive wound modifier against high-Strength attacks.

Switching mode. You start in Hostile Acquisition. At the end of your Command phase, if you have 7+ YP, Fortify Takeover becomes active until your next Command phase; otherwise Hostile Acquisition remains active.

Force DispositionsMISSION

This army: Purge the Foe + Reconnaissance.

Practical reminder. Purge the Foe rewards converting kills; Reconnaissance rewards board movement, early pressure, and keeping utility pieces alive long enough to score.

Farseekers · Eye of the HuntFARSEEKERS

Detachment Rule. Friendly HERNKYN units’ ranged attacks that target a unit within 12" have +1 to hit rolls.

Pan-spectral Lockons · Upgrade. PIONEERS unit only. In your Shooting phase, select one visible enemy unit within 12" of this unit. That enemy unit is spotted. While a unit is spotted, that unit has +3" detection range.

Shroudwërke Talismans · Upgrade. YAEGIRS unit only. This unit has -3" detection range.

Stratagems

Core Stratagems plus Needgaârd Oathband and Farseekers. No Hearthguard Covenant stratagems.

Command Re-roll1CPCore Stratagem

WHEN: Any phase, just after you make a Hit roll, Wound roll, Damage roll, saving throw, Advance roll, Charge roll or Battle-shock test. TARGET: That roll or test. EFFECT: Re-roll it.

Epic Challenge1CPCore Stratagem

WHEN: Fight phase, when a CHARACTER model in your unit that is within Engagement Range of one or more Attached units is selected to fight. TARGET: That CHARACTER model. EFFECT: Until the end of the phase, melee attacks made by that model have PRECISION.

Insane Bravery1CPCore Stratagem

WHEN: Battle-shock step of your Command phase, just before you take a Battle-shock test for a unit. TARGET: That unit. EFFECT: That unit automatically passes that test. You can only use this Stratagem once per battle.

Explosives1CPCore Stratagem

WHEN: Your Shooting phase. TARGET: One GRENADES unit from your army that has not been selected to shoot this phase and is not within Engagement Range of enemy units. EFFECT: Select one enemy unit within 8" and visible to your unit. Roll six D6: for each 4+, that enemy unit suffers 1 mortal wound.

Crushing Impact1CPCore Stratagem

WHEN: Your Charge phase, after a VEHICLE unit from your army ends a Charge move. TARGET: That VEHICLE unit. EFFECT: Select one enemy unit within Engagement Range of your unit and resolve Crushing Impact as described in the Core Rules.

Rapid Ingress1CPCore Stratagem

WHEN: End of your opponent’s Movement phase. TARGET: One unit from your army that is in Reserves. EFFECT: Your unit can arrive as if it were the Reinforcements step of your Movement phase, following all normal placement restrictions.

Fire Overwatch1CPCore Stratagem

WHEN: Your opponent’s Movement or Charge phase, just after an enemy unit is set up or starts/ends a Normal, Advance, Fall Back or Charge move. TARGET: One unit from your army within 24" of that enemy unit and eligible to shoot. EFFECT: Shoot that enemy unit as if it were your Shooting phase. Unmodified Hit rolls of 6 are required, unless stated otherwise.

Smokescreen1CPCore Stratagem

WHEN: Your opponent’s Shooting phase, when an enemy unit selects its targets. TARGET: One SMOKE unit from your army that was selected as the target of one or more attacks. EFFECT: Until the end of the phase, attacks that target your unit subtract 1 from the Hit roll and your unit has Benefit of Cover.

Heroic Intervention1CPCore Stratagem

WHEN: Your opponent’s Charge phase, just after an enemy unit ends a Charge move. TARGET: One unit from your army that is within 6" of that enemy unit and would be eligible to declare a charge against it. EFFECT: Your unit declares a charge against that enemy unit. It does not receive any Charge bonus this turn.

Counter-offensive2CPCore Stratagem

WHEN: Fight phase, just after an enemy unit has fought. TARGET: One unit from your army that is within Engagement Range of one or more enemy units and has not fought this phase. EFFECT: Your unit fights next.

Void Hardened1CPNeedgaârd Oathband · Wargear Stratagem

WHEN: Your opponent’s Shooting phase or the Fight phase, just after an enemy unit has selected its targets, if units from your army have Fortify Takeover. TARGET: One LEAGUES OF VOTANN unit from your army that was selected as the target of one or more of the attacking unit’s attacks. EFFECT: Until the attacking unit has finished making its attacks, each time an attack targets your unit, worsen the Armour Penetration characteristic of that attack by 1.

Honour of the Hold1CPNeedgaârd Oathband · Battle Tactic Stratagem

WHEN: Fight phase. TARGET: One LEAGUES OF VOTANN unit from your army that has not been selected to fight this phase. EFFECT: You can spend 3YP. Select one enemy unit within Engagement Range of your unit. Until the end of the phase, melee attacks made by your unit that target that enemy unit improve AP by 1. If you spent YP, improve AP by 2 instead.

Ordered Retreat1CPNeedgaârd Oathband · Strategic Ploy Stratagem

WHEN: Your Movement phase, just after a LEAGUES OF VOTANN unit from your army Falls Back. TARGET: That LEAGUES OF VOTANN unit. EFFECT: Until the end of the turn, your unit is eligible to shoot and declare a charge in a turn in which it Fell Back.

Ancestral Sentence1CPNeedgaârd Oathband · Battle Tactic Stratagem

WHEN: Your Shooting phase. TARGET: One LEAGUES OF VOTANN unit from your army that has not been selected to shoot this phase. EFFECT: You can spend 3YP. Until the end of the phase, ranged weapons equipped by models in your unit have SUSTAINED HITS 1. If you spent YP, they have SUSTAINED HITS 2 instead.

HunTR’s Mark1CPNeedgaârd Oathband · Battle Tactic Stratagem

WHEN: Your Shooting phase. TARGET: One LEAGUES OF VOTANN unit from your army that has not been selected to shoot this phase. EFFECT: Until the end of the phase, each time a model in your unit makes an attack, re-roll a Hit roll of 1 and re-roll a Wound roll of 1.

Reactive Reprisal2CPNeedgaârd Oathband · Strategic Ploy Stratagem

WHEN: Your opponent’s Shooting phase, just after an enemy unit has shot, if units from your army have Fortify Takeover. TARGET: One LEAGUES OF VOTANN unit from your army that was selected as the target of one or more of the attacking unit’s attacks. EFFECT: Your unit can shoot as if it were your Shooting phase, can only target that enemy unit when doing so, and can only do so if that enemy unit is an eligible target.

Scornful Analysis1CPFarseekers Stratagem

WHEN: Start of your Shooting phase. TARGET: One friendly HERNKYN unit. EFFECT: Select one visible enemy unit within 12" of your unit. Friendly LEAGUES OF VOTANN units’ attacks that target that enemy unit have IGNORES COVER.

No Shot Wasted1CPFarseekers Stratagem

WHEN: Your Shooting phase, when a friendly HERNKYN unit is selected to shoot. TARGET: That HERNKYN unit. EFFECT: Your unit’s ranged attacks have LETHAL HITS.

Economy of Motion1CPFarseekers Stratagem

WHEN: Your opponent’s Movement phase, when an enemy unit ends a move within 8" of a friendly unengaged PIONEERS unit. TARGET: That PIONEERS unit. EFFECT: Your unit can make a Normal move of up to D3+3".
